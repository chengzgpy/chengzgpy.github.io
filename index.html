What we do

.js-id-Deep-Learning

  1. Optoelectronics

Optoelectronics is the study and application of electronic devices that interact with light (visible, the infrared or ultraviolet region), which are used in a wide variety of application areas, such as optical fiber communications, laser technology, and all kinds of optical metrology. Typical devices are photodetectors and electro-optic modulators.

  1. Programmable Photonics

Today, most of photonic circuits are designed for a specific application, but the increase in complexity has introduced a generation of photonic circuits that can be programmed using software for a wide variety of functions through a mesh of on-chip waveguides, tunable beam couplers and optical phase shifters.

  1. Photonic Computing

Implementing signal processing in the optical domain benefiting from the high speed and unprecedented bandwidth could eventually address the von Neumann bottleneck between the central processing unit and the memory in the existing computing architecture, especially driven by the request for boosting speed along with minimal power consumption.
